# Privacy Policy for SpeleoTrace

**Effective Date:** 4/1/2026

Chris Berthold built SpeleoTrace as an Open Source app. This SERVICE is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
For a better experience, while using our Service, I may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:
* **Precise Location Data:** Used for radio location triangulation.
* **Compass/Sensor Data:** Used to determine device orientation for triangulation.

**Important:** The information that I request will be retained on your device and is NOT collected, stored, or transmitted to any external servers or third parties by me in any way.

## 2. Permissions
To provide the core functionality of radio location triangulation, the app requires:
* **Location Permission:** To access GPS coordinates.
* **Sensor Access:** To read the device's compass/magnetometer.

## 3. Third-Party Services
This app does not use third-party services that collect information used to identify you (e.g., no ads, no analytics, no crash reporting).

## 4. Children’s Privacy
These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
I may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. 

## 6. Contact Us
